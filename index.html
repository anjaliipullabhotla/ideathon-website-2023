WHAT IS IT?

Ideathon 2023 will be kicking off Friday, March 31st @ 5 PM in the IOP living room @ 5 PM in the IOP living room (5707 S. Woodlawn Avenue). Between the kick-off and the weekend event (April 22nd-23rd), participants will have the opportunity to workshop ideas at IOP fellow Adrian Perkin's office hours as well as attending a technical workshop! Participants will identify and propose creative solutions to some of the major issues in tech and policy. These solutions could take the form of a government policy/tax/subsidy, app, program, educational class—anything! Absolutely feel free to think outside the box! The winning team will be awarded a $250 grand prize per participant!!

On the morning of Sunday, April 23rd, participants will give a live presentation on the problem you are addressing and your solution. We will provide detailed guidelines for what we are looking for in your slide deck. Please submit all relevant links here.

WHO IS IT FOR?

Anyone who attends the University of Chicago! We want to create an open space for discussion and thoughtful collaboration across all majors and schools.

We are asking for teams of up to 5 students, including individual participants. At the time of registration, you may register as a team or as an individual. During the Ideathon, we will match individuals who requested to be part of a team with a group.

WHERE IS IT?

We have space booked in the Polsky Center (1452 E 53rd Street, Chicago IL) for speaker events and work sessions during the weekend.

TELL ME MORE!

No technical or coding skills necessary! We encourage students with a variety of skills, interests, and backgrounds to sign up. Before and during the event, please look through this scoping document to guide your brainstorming of policy and technical solutions to one of the two themes!
