SCHEDULE

IOP Living Room, 5707 S. Woodlawn Avenue, Chicago IL 60637 March 31st, 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Kick Off Presentation

We'll be kicking off Ideathon 2023 by providing an overview of this year's three topics: (1) Restorative Justice, (2) Smart City Initiatives, (3) the Urban-Rural Divide. We'll be previewing the amazing people who have volunteered to be speakers, mentors, and judges for this year. We'll also be providing tips on successful presentations, including winning submissions from previous years. We'll also show how to sign up for office hours with Mayor Perkins and sign up for a technical workshop lead by TechTeam members!

David Rubenstein Forum, 1201 E 60th St, Chicago IL 60637 April 23, 1:00–2:00 PM

Fireside Chat with Alicia Rouault

Enjoy a fireside chat with Alicia Rouault, a Product Manager at the United States Digital Service. She has previously served as the Director of Product at 18F, a digital consultancy within the US government with the Technology Transformation Service at GSA. Learn more about her work on her personal website.

David Rubenstein Forum, 1201 E 60th St, Chicago IL 60637 April 23, 2:00–8:00 PM

Small group work time and mentor office hours

Continue working with your group on your tech proposals in the Rubenstein forum, with mentor office hours available to gain feedback on your proposals or ask questions about their work in civic tech, etc. Dinner will also be catered. Please remember to submit your proposals by 8 PM on Saturday, April 23rd.

5707 S Woodlawn Ave, Chicago, IL 60637 April 23, 8:30PM–9:30 PM

Ice Cream Social in the IOP Living Room

Relax with your group and mingle with the other participants for a chill ice cream social.

David Rubenstein Forum, 1201 E 60th St, Chicago IL 60637 April 24, 11:00 AM–1:00 PM

April 24th: Closing Ceremony

Groups will get to present their proposals and winners will be annouced, with coffee and bagels catered.
